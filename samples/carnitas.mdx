<IngredientGroup title="Pork">
- 4 lb pork shoulder, large chunks
- 1 orange, peel only
- 1 onion, quartered
</IngredientGroup>

<IngredientGroup title="Aromatics">
- 6 clove garlic, smashed
- 2 bay leaves
</IngredientGroup>

<IngredientGroup title="Fat">
- lard, enough to cover
</IngredientGroup>

<Step timer="7200">Simmer pork low in lard with aromatics until tender but not shredded.</Step>
<Step timer="300">Broil to crisp edges just before serving.</Step>
